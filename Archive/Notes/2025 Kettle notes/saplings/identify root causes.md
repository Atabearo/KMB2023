After the "[[identify problem]]" step, it is time to identify the *why* of the problem.
### Root cause analysis tools
If it is a simple to semi-difficult problem where the scope is limited to a single line of inquiry, it may be solved by asking why ([[the 5 Whys]]).

If it is a complex issue like climate change or national opioid addiction, where root causes split into multiple tracks of inquiry with their own root causes, a more complex cause-and-effect model will be needed.
